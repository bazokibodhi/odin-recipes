Chickpea Salad

Homepage

Description

A chickpea salad is basically a versatile and nutritious dish featuring chickpeas as the main ingredient

It is often mixed with vegetables, herbs and other tangy or (and) spicy stuffs

These are extremely healthy and very easy to customize

These are mixed with sandwitches, salad or consumed on it's own

Ingredients

  • 2 (19 ounce) cans chickpeas (garbanzos), drained and rinsed
  • 1 pint small cherry tomatoes, halved
  • ½ cup crumbled goat-milk feta chees
  • 3 tablespoons finely shredded basil leaves
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 3 large cloves garclic minced
  • 3 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 3 tablespoons cider vinegar
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • ¼ teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
