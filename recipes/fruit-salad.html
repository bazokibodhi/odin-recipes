Summer Fruit Salad

Homepage

Description

A summer fruit salad is a refreshing dish that contains multiple fruits like berries, melons and tropical fruits

It is a popular choice among a huge number of population for the need of a light and cool dessert or snack

The combination of various fruits provide a variety of flavours and textures making it enjoyable and visually appealing dish

The key is using the fruits that are seasonal and at the very peak of their ripeness

Ingredients

  • Fresh Oranges
  • Lemon Juice
  • Brown Sugar
  • Vanilla Extracts
  • Pineapples
  • strawberries
  • Kiwi
  • Banana
  • Grapes
  • Blue Berries

Steps to make a fruit salad

  1. Make the sauce on the stove and let it cool.
  2. Arrange the fruits in a container, then pour the sauce over them.
  3. Cover and refrigerate to allow the flavors to meld.
